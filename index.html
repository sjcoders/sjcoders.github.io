An Educational Experience

Ever wanted to learn how to write code?

SJ Coders offers a fun, beginner-friendly curriculum inspired by top universities to young students that conventional schools do not provide. We empower students to think critically and build a foundation of knowledge that allows them to start a career in Software Engineering or Computer Science.

Project-Driven

We provide a project-driven curriculum that allows students to get hands-on experience with developing real projects, nurturing their growth in software development through personalized feedback.

Experienced Instructors

Our instructors attend top universities for Computer Science such as UCLA and University of Maryland, where they have done research in academia. They have also held internships at top tech companies, such as Amazon Web Services, VMware, Tesla, and Symantec.

We facilitate high, individual interaction between the instructors and students in order for students to be able to synthesize feedback and ask any questions they may have regarding career development or navigating through college.
