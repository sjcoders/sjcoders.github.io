Intro to Java

About the course

Our course content is modeled after the nation's top universities and the AP Computer Science A curriculum.
Not only do we teach the basics of Java, but we also use industry standard tools. Your child will not only pick up programming, but also get an introduction on software engineering!
This course is great for middle - high school students. Some experience with programming is handy, but not at all necessary.
