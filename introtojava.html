Intro to Java

About the course

Our course content is modeled after the nation's top universities and the AP Computer Science A curriculum.
Not only do we teach the basics of Java, but we also use industry standard tools. Your child will not only pick up programming, but also get an introduction on software engineering!
This course is great for middle - high school students. Some experience with programming is handy, but not at all necessary.

What sets us apart

  • Project Driven - We assign and grade projects through our unique workflows which help our students learn better and learn more.
  • High Interaction - Apart from our weekly online class, we are available throughout the week for students via email and slack.
  • Advanced Curriculum - Our content is influenced by the top universities and covers AP Computer Science A.
  • Experienced Instructors - Our instructors are from top ranked universities and have industry experience in the top software companies.
